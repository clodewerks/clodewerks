Clodewerks Releases Clodewerks 3: Kyle's Smiles, A Lush Electronic Trumpet Record Built Over Years of Experimentation

Portland, OR – Electronic trumpet artist Clodewerks returns with a new album, Clodewerks 3: Kyle's Smiles, a collection of richly layered instrumental tracks blending trumpet, electronics, and immersive sound design.

Rather than being written as a single project, Clodewerks 3 grew organically over several years. The album brings together pieces that evolved through live performance alongside studio experiments that gradually took shape through refinement and revisiting older ideas.

The result is a record that captures the core of the Clodewerks sound: expressive trumpet melodies floating through dense electronic textures, looping harmonies, and atmospheric production.

Some tracks have been part of Clodewerks live sets for years, while others were ideas that lingered unfinished until recently. Revisiting them with fresh ears allowed them to finally click into place, forming a cohesive record that feels both reflective and forward-looking.

Performing under the name Clodewerks, Portland-based trumpeter and producer Kyle Linneman combines live looping, improvisation, and electronic production to create layered instrumental music that sits somewhere between ambient, experimental electronic, and modern jazz.

Kyle's Smiles continues that exploration, highlighting the warmth of trumpet against evolving electronic landscapes.

The album will be released digitally in late April 2026.

About Clodewerks

Clodewerks is the musical moniker of Kyle Linneman, an accomplished trumpet player and innovative producer. With a rich background in trumpet performance and a keen interest in electronic music, Kyle crafts a lush and unique sound that bridges the gap between the organic and the synthetic.

Live, Clodewerks offers an immersive experience by seamlessly blending looping, samples, and live effects. Each performance is a dynamic and engaging journey that captivates audiences and showcases Kyle’s technical prowess and creative vision.

With over 20 years of trumpet performance experience, Kyle holds a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. His diverse musical journey includes classical and jazz trumpet performances, as well as producing cutting-edge electronic music. He is also a valued member of several musical ensembles, including Waffle Taco, Couch Brass, and the Stochastic Mettle Union.

As Clodewerks, Kyle has released three full-length albums and four EPs, each showcasing his evolving sound and innovative approach to music production. His live performances have graced stages at notable events such as NW Loopfest and Milwaukie Porchfest, earning him a dedicated following and critical acclaim.

Whether in the studio or on stage, Clodewerks is a testament to Kyle Linneman’s dedication to pushing musical boundaries and creating unforgettable auditory experiences.
