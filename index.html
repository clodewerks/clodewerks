About

Clodewerks is Kyle Linneman, trumpet player and producer. Kyle combines his passion for trumpet and electronic music into a lush and unique sound. Live, Clodewerks combines looping, samples, and live effects to make an immersive experience.

Kyle has 20 years of trumpet performance experience. He has a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. He has played classical and jazz, and produced electronic music. Kyle also performs with Waffle Taco, the Montavilla Brass Quintet, and the Stochastic Mettle Union .

Clodewerks has two albums and an EP, and has performed at NW Loopfest.
