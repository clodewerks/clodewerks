About

Clodewerks is the musical moniker of Kyle Linneman, an accomplished trumpet player and innovative producer. With a rich background in trumpet performance and a keen interest in electronic music, Kyle crafts a lush and unique sound that bridges the gap between the organic and the synthetic.

Live, Clodewerks offers an immersive experience by seamlessly blending looping, samples, and live effects. Each performance is a dynamic and engaging journey that captivates audiences and showcases Kyle’s technical prowess and creative vision.

With over 20 years of trumpet performance experience, Kyle holds a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. His diverse musical journey includes classical and jazz trumpet performances, as well as producing cutting-edge electronic music. He is also a valued member of several musical ensembles, including Waffle Taco, Couch Brass, and the Stochastic Mettle Union.

As Clodewerks, Kyle has released three full-length albums and four EPs, each showcasing his evolving sound and innovative approach to music production. His live performances have graced stages at notable events such as NW Loopfest and Milwaukie Porchfest, earning him a dedicated following and critical acclaim.

Whether in the studio or on stage, Clodewerks is a testament to Kyle Linneman’s dedication to pushing musical boundaries and creating unforgettable auditory experiences.
