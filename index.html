About

Clodewerks is Kyle Linneman, trumpet player and producer. Kyle combines his passion for trumpet and electronic music into a lush and unique sound. Live, Clodewerks combines looping, samples, and live effects to make an immersive experience.

Kyle has 20 years of trumpet performance experience, and has a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. He has played classical and jazz, and produced electronic music. In addition to playing solo, Kyle performs with jazz duo Waffle Taco, the Montavilla Brass Quintet, and Stochastic Mettle Union Local #35.
