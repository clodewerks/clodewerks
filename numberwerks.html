Clodewerks Announces the Release of New EP "Numberwerks"

Portland, OR – 8/12/2024 – Clodewerks, the innovative musical alias of Kyle Linneman, is set to release a new EP titled "Numberwerks" on September 6th. This highly anticipated release marks another milestone in Clodewerks' evolving journey through the realms of electronic and experimental music.

"Numberwerks" features a captivating collection of pure trumpet compositions, showcasing Clodewerks' mastery of the instrument and his innovative approach to experimental music. Stripping away electronic elements, this EP highlights intricate melodies and harmonies crafted through unconventional compositional techniques. The result is a raw and authentic listening experience that pushes the boundaries of traditional trumpet music.

With over 20 years of trumpet performance experience and a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon, Kyle Linneman has established himself as a versatile and visionary artist. Under the Clodewerks moniker, he has released three albums and two EPs, garnering acclaim for his boundary-pushing compositions and dynamic live performances. Clodewerks has graced the stages of notable events such as NW Loopfest and Milwaukie Porchfest, captivating audiences with his innovative approach to music.

The release of "Numberwerks" is a testament to Clodewerks' relentless pursuit of musical excellence and creative exploration. Each track on the EP offers a glimpse into Linneman's artistic process, showcasing his ability to seamlessly blend traditional instrumentation with modern electronic elements.

About Clodewerks

Clodewerks is the musical moniker of Kyle Linneman, an accomplished trumpet player and innovative producer. With a rich background in trumpet performance and a keen interest in electronic music, Kyle crafts a lush and unique sound that bridges the gap between the organic and the synthetic.

Live, Clodewerks offers an immersive experience by seamlessly blending looping, samples, and live effects. Each performance is a dynamic and engaging journey that captivates audiences and showcases Kyle’s technical prowess and creative vision.

With over 20 years of trumpet performance experience, Kyle holds a degree in Music Technology from the University of Oregon. His diverse musical journey includes classical and jazz trumpet performances, as well as producing cutting-edge electronic music. He is also a valued member of several musical ensembles, including Waffle Taco, Couch Brass, and the Stochastic Mettle Union.

As Clodewerks, Kyle has released three full-length albums and four EPs, each showcasing his evolving sound and innovative approach to music production. His live performances have graced stages at notable events such as NW Loopfest and Milwaukie Porchfest, earning him a dedicated following and critical acclaim.

Whether in the studio or on stage, Clodewerks is a testament to Kyle Linneman’s dedication to pushing musical boundaries and creating unforgettable auditory experiences.
